How does it work ?

Hepatitis A and B is an infectious disease that is caused by viral infection usually in a contaminated water or even food, the virus is deadly as it affects the Liver causing bilirubin levels to reduce and causing other complications even leading to death. The Kenya Center for Disease Control (KCDC) has a procedure in dealing with Infections diseases my administering the County to take Reports of monthly number of cases. .

This model predicts infectious diseases by optimizing the parameters of machine learning algorithms while considering big data of previous diagnosis. The performance of the deep neural network is due to previous diagnosis of such disease hence reliable data is key to the accuracy of this system. When predicting Hepatitis, A and B. Using a Gaussian Naïve Bayes classification model to do predictions can also do necessary actions and precautions to counter the infection rate in our country especially in our villages.
